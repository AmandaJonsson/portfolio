Creating personas

This persona project was a summer project that continued into the fall 2019. I and a colleague worked in this project together. We gathered data in terms of interviews. We interviewed twentyfour persons in sweden and in UK. We analyzed the data with the method affinity diagramming, showed in the images below. The project also involved introducing the personas to the company. To give them a tool to become a more user-centered company.
