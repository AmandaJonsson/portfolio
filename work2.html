Grab(it)y

Grab(it)y, an interactive and explorative game where the players experience the gravitational force at Mars. The project was developed in collaboration with the Chalmers University of Technology and Universeum and exhibited on the 25th and 26th of October 2019 at Universeum, Gothenburg, Sweden. The theme of the project was ‘showing the invisible’ and the target group were kids between the ages of 12-16.
