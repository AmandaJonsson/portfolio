DESIGN FOR ANDROID

This was a part of a course in my master program. The task was to design an application for Android that families with children could use to rent things from each other. Due to that the application should be designed for Android we needed to read up on the guidelines that Android uses. So I designed this application with the Material Design Guidelines in mind.
